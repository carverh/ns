## Minimum Requirements
<ul>
<li>32-bit x86 CPU with SSE and SSE2 extentions</li>
<li>50MB RAM</li>
<li>800x600x32 VESA compatible video card</li>
<li>CD-ROM Drive</li>
</ul>
